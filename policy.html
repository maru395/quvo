Cookies are small data files commonly used as anonymous unique identifiers. They are sent to your browser from the website you visit and stored on your device.

Our website uses cookies to collect information and improve your experience. You have the option to accept or refuse these cookies and to be notified when a cookie is being sent to your device. If you choose to refuse cookies, some portions of our services may not function correctly.
